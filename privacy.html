Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026  ·  Applies to awrylabs.com

In brief

awrylabs.com uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. We don't know you visited. That's by design.

Data We Collect

None. This website does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

There are no analytics scripts, no session tracking, no advertising pixels, no fingerprinting, and no third-party SDKs of any kind embedded in this site. It's static HTML. It loads, you read it, nothing is recorded.

Cookies

No cookies are set. There is no cookie banner because there is nothing to consent to.

Third-Party Scripts

None. No external JavaScript, no CDN-hosted libraries, no social embeds, no comment systems. Every asset on this site is served directly — no third party can track your visit through a resource load.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server-level access logs (IP address, request timestamp, user agent) as part of standard web infrastructure. This is outside awryLabs' control. GitHub's privacy policy applies: docs.github.com.

External Links

This site links to third-party destinations such as the Apple App Store. Those destinations have their own data practices — we don't control what happens once you leave awrylabs.com.

App Privacy

Each awryLabs app has its own privacy policy covering what happens on-device, in iCloud, and through any platform services (Game Center, StoreKit, etc.). Links to individual app privacy policies are available on each app's page.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our practices in a way that warrants updating this page, we will. The effective date at the top will reflect it. Given that the policy is "collect nothing," material changes seem unlikely.
